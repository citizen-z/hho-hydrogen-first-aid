A vital part of any Hydrogen on demand system is it’s safety features. Due to the violent nature of HHO gas we can’t afford a flash back to reach the electrolyzer. The most common solution is The HHO bubbler.

Another interesting option is the Flame Arrestor. A simple assembly that prevents from flash backs reaching the electrolyzer.

 

Don’t compromise on safety.
