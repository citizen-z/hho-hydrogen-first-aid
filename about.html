We are a group of people devoted to make low consumption technologies to standard in all cars. We seen it is not a dream and for unknown reasons the car companies refuse to include those technology is new cars.

Me and my friends, many times, treat to achieving high MPG like an art - it works only when everything is perfect. Luckily, we have the formal education to achieve our goals. We decided to start this site and to share our knowledge and experience to increases awareness to hydrogen on demand technology and give the people good tools to start and experimenting on their own.

We believe that it is wrong to ruin our planet for couple of dollars.

We think that it is illogical to take 1.5 ton of steel every time someone drives to work, but this is the reality and we work to make things better.

Oil is running out and no real alternative is near, prices jump up and down like rutted rabbit. A change must come and fast.
